Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

At Vela Vida, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data we hold about you. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you book a sailing expedition with us.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you inquire or book:

  • Identity Data: Name, passport details (for marina/charter registration).
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number.
  • Health/Safety Data: Dietary requirements, relevant medical conditions for safety at sea.

3. How We Use Your Data

We strictly use your data to:

  • Process your booking and payments.
  • Register you with local port authorities and charter companies (legal requirement).
  • Ensure your safety onboard.
  • Send you trip details and logistics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share necessary details with:
- The Charter Company (for crew lists).
- Port Authorities (as required by maritime law).
